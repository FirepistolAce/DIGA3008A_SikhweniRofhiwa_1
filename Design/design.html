Designs

Documentation of IDX Process

My goal for this website remains the same: to create a website that is user-friendly and accessible, while also being unique and enticing. I acknowledge that this goal may change over time as I grow as a web designer. For my information structure, I opted for a more generic layout to guide users smoothly—from the Profile (to read about the creator), to the Portfolio (to view my work), then to the Design page (to explain design decisions), and finally to Blogs and Essays (for documentation of the journey and adversities faced).

For user flow, I want clarity from the homepage. Each page has a visible header so users know where they are, along with a top navigation menu for free movement across pages. Additionally, I plan to have navigation buttons at the bottom of pages for sequential flow. I might later choose between direct links or buttons—currently, each serves a different purpose: quick access vs guided flow.

I chose the current color scheme because I am a huge fan of the anime Naruto. The design mimics its most prominent colors: green/dark green, blue, and orange.
